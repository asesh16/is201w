About Me

Here are some of my favorite activities
  1. Hiking
    • I love to backpack. My favorite place to backpack in the Big Horn Mountains in Wyoming.
    • My whole family has loved to hike my whole life. We go together everytime I go home.
  2. Fishing
    • Fishing is my favorite activity to do in my free time.
    • I prefer to fly fish, but I also like to fish with a spinning rod and ice fish when the lakes are frozen.
    • I mainly catch trout, but sometimes I bowfish for carp.
  3. Golf
    • I have played golf for most of my life.
    • I was on the varsity golf team when I was in high school.
    • I don't play nearly as much anymore but I do try to get out every once in a while. I am not quite as good as I used to be.
